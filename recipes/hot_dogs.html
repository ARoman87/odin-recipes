Hot Dog Bourbon Appetizers

It can't get any easier than this. Serve these hot dog appetizers at fancy occasions such as weddings.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package hot dogs, cut into 1-inch pieces.
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup bourbon

Steps

  1. Mix hot dogs, brown sugar, ketchup, and bourbon in a saucepan; bring to a boil. Cook, stirring occasionally, until hot dogs are cooked through and evenly coated, about 10 minutes. Transfer to a slow cooker to keep warm for serving.
